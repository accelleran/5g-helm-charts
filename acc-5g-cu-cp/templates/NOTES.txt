Release notes:
# Added
  - Added __APPID to ds-ctrl, e1-ap, f1-ap, ng-ap and rr-ctrl
  - Micro Services Failover: Recovery mechanism for a micro service which restarts while in service. 3 CU-CP micro services (f1apIntf, e1cpIntf and ngapIntf) included in this release.
  - KPI SM Support
  - NR SIB Information: Support for SIB2, SIB3, SIB4 and SIB5
  - Ethernet and Unstructured PDU Sessions: Support for additional pdu session types
  - CU KPIs added
  - Support for dynamic reconfiguration via Netconf
